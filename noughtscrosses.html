Noughts and Crosses Game

Rules and Instructions

The object of Noughts and Crosses is to get a winning line of three Noughts or three Crosses in either a horizontal, vertical or diagonal row. Each player takes it in turns to place their piece, a nought or a cross, and whoever lines up 3 first wins!
